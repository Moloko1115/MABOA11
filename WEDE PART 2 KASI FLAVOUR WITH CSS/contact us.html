Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Thursday from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Friday and Saturday from 7:00 AM to midnight, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Do you offer delivery?

Currently, we operate on a walk-in basis. However, you can contact us via WhatsApp or phone to inquire about special arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash. For specific inquiries about other payment methods, please contact us directly.

Can I place a bulk order for an event?

Yes! We can accommodate bulk orders for events and gatherings. Please contact us via phone or email at least 24 hours in advance to discuss your requirements.

Are there vegetarian options available?

Yes, we offer vegetarian options such as Pap with Chakalaka and our Magwinya (Vetkoek). For more details, feel free to contact us.

Where is your restaurant located?

We are located at The Greenacres Shopping Centre, 3rd Entrance, Port Elizabeth, South Africa. You can view our location on Google Maps using the link in the navigation menu.
