KASI FLAVOUR is a small, locally owned township restaurant that specialises in authentic South African kasi food such as mogodu, pap, chakalaka, and grilled meat. The business was started by a passionate cook who wanted to share traditional home-style meals with the community at affordable prices. The restaurant currently operates through walk-in customers and informal marketing such as word-of-mouth and social media.

Our mission is to provide affordable, authentic, and delicious kasi meals while promoting local culture and community spirit.
